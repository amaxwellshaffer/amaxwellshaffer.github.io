About Me

I have had a passion for Visual arts for as long as I can remember.
I studied Video Production and Print Design at Ball State University. This has led me to my current career as a Graphic Artist.
I recently won a national award for creative trophy design. I enjoy applying my creative sense to meet clients' needs.
In keeping up with the industry and the times, I am currently studying Web Development at 1150 Academy. It is my hope to always be learning new skills and media with which to express myself and communicate with others.
